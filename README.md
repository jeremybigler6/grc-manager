# GRC Manager

A Python-based Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) application that simulates the core functionality of a commercial risk management platform. This project was built to strengthen my understanding of GRC concepts while improving my Python programming skills.

## Features

### Risk Management

* Create, view, edit, and delete risks
* Search for risks
* View detailed Risk Profiles
* Track likelihood, impact, risk score, and risk level
* Assign owners and treatment plans
* Track risk status and completion dates

### Control Management

* Create, view, edit, and delete controls
* Search for controls
* Link multiple controls to a single risk
* View all controls associated with a risk

### Reporting & Analysis

* Risk Dashboard
* Risk statistics
* Open vs. closed risks
* Risk level distribution
* Average and highest risk scores
* Upcoming and overdue risks
* Risk heat map
